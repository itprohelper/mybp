Yakahu is a test app

This app1 is NOT meant to replace your doctor's advice, your own or the 'official' high blood pressure standards.

The main purpose of this app is to keep a record of your blood pressure readings. You must use a blood pressure monitor to gather systolic and diastolic readings. I developed this simple app to keep my own blood pressure readings and share them with my doctor.

I found I was having White Collar Syndrome Blood Pressure. This led me to have a way to keep a BP record at home.

Many thanks to ITPro Helper for the inspiration.
